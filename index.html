ABOUT:

Hi, I'm Wahib. Welcome to my portfolio for my Communications Lab class! The theme of this portfolio has been inspired by truck-art prints, which are a vital component of the Pakistani culture. Use your up and down arrow keys to scroll below for a journey full of colors as you explore each of my four projects for the class represented by a unique truck art print. Hover over the top of the print for a sneak peak of the project or click on the print to get redirected to the website of the project!
