About the film
The Hungry Games is an iconic production nominated as the Best Short Film at the 30MFF - 30 Minute Film Festival. The aim of the movie is to enlighten the society regarding the everyday struggles of college students. From power-naps during classes to food adventures in-between breaks and the subsequent rush to class as a result of time contraint, this film is packed with fun-filled yet suspenseful moments encountered by Wahib and Atoka on their Dining Hall venture!
